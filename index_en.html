Welcome

W
elcome to my personal homepage!

My name is Gerasimos Lampouras, I graduated from the Athens Univercity of Economics and Business department of Informatics with a degree in Computer Science (2002 - 2005, grade: 7,22/10). After that I got an M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the same University (2006 - 2007, grade: 8,58/10). I am currently employed as a Research Assistant in the research program Indigo and part of the Natural Language Processing Group.

Both my theses were on Question Answering systems, namely systems that receive questions from the user and try to return an answer for them usually by searching for candidate answers in the World Wide Web or large collections of documents. Generally I am interested in Natural Language Processing problems and anything about Artificial Intelligence, logic, computational complexity and various infinities.

My personal interests include drawing/painting (got my own little webcomic running), roleplaying, reading books (mostly fantasy but also history/philosophy/mystery ones), computer gaming (trapped in the various blizzard universes), and of' course music (Delivorias, Pasxalidis, Maxairitsas and anything to do with Southern Cross and Nikos Kavvadias being amongst my Greek favorites). And still more...can't list everything!
